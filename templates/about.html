Who We Are

PropelHub is a vibrant community of passionate students, innovators, and aviation enthusiasts at BMS College of Engineering. Founded in 2025, our club is dedicated to exploring, designing, and flying model aircraft and drones. We bring together curious minds from diverse backgrounds to learn, build, and soar—both literally and figuratively!

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students to innovate, collaborate, and push the boundaries of aeromodelling and drone technology. We aim to foster hands-on learning, creativity, and teamwork through exciting projects, workshops, and competitions.

What We Do

  • Design & Build: We conceptualize, design, and construct model aircraft and drones, applying real engineering principles and creativity.
  • Workshops & Training: We regularly host workshops, guest lectures, and training sessions on aerodynamics, electronics, flight control, and more.
  • Competitions: Our teams participate in national and international aeromodelling and drone competitions, consistently achieving top rankings.
  • Research & Innovation: We encourage research in new technologies, from autonomous flight to advanced materials and control systems.
  • Community & Outreach: We inspire and mentor the next generation through school outreach, airshows, and STEM events.

Why Join PropelHub?

  • Hands-on Experience: Gain practical skills in design, fabrication, and piloting.
  • Collaborative Environment: Work with like-minded peers and experienced mentors.
  • Career Boost: Build a strong portfolio for careers in aerospace, robotics, and technology.
  • Fun & Friendship: Experience the thrill of flight and make lifelong friends!

Our Values

  • Innovation: We believe in pushing boundaries and thinking outside the box.
  • Teamwork: We achieve more when we work together.
  • Learning: Every project is a new opportunity to grow.
  • Inclusion: All students, regardless of background or experience, are welcome.

Get Involved

Whether you’re a seasoned flyer or just curious about aeromodelling and drones, PropelHub has a place for you!
Join us and let’s build, fly, and innovate together.
